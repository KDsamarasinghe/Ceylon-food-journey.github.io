Chicken Curry Recipe

Experience the delicious flavors of Sri Lankan Chicken Curry.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, sliced
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4 cardamom pods
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons Sri Lankan curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • Salt to taste

Cooking Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot. Add onions, garlic, ginger, green chilies, and curry leaves. Saut√© until the onions are golden brown.
  2. Add cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, and cook for a minute until fragrant.
  3. Stir in curry powder and turmeric powder. Cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  4. Add chicken pieces and cook until they are browned on all sides.
  5. Add diced tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  6. Pour in coconut milk, season with salt, and bring to a simmer. Cover and let it cook until the chicken is tender.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice or roti.
