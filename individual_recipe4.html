Egg Hoppers Recipe

Indulge in the delightful taste of Sri Lankan Egg Hoppers.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 4 eggs
  • Salt to taste
  • Vegetable oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix rice flour, all-purpose flour, coconut milk, yeast, sugar, and enough water to form a batter. Let it ferment for a few hours.
  2. Add baking soda and salt to the batter. Mix well.
  3. Heat a hopper pan or a non-stick pan. Pour a ladle of batter into the pan and swirl to coat the edges.
  4. Crack an egg into the center of the hopper. Cover and cook until the edges are crispy, and the egg is cooked to your liking.
  5. Serve hot with chicken curry or sambol.
